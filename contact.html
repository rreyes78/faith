The factory is located at Purok Mahogany 2, Brgy. Poblacion, Liloan, where Holy Candle Factory was based. The factory was positioned near the corner street with the signage of Manila Memorial Park. It was accessible via a small road leading to Manila Memorial Park’s entrance. From there, the factory was located by a small road to the left at another corner street. It was situated at the dead-end of this road and was identifiable by its silver gate.
